ABOUT TAKSHAK

Takshak, MACE’s annual tech fest, derives its name from the giant five-headed serpent king. And just like the naga proudly rears its head, Takshak holds the label of being one among the premier techie meets of South India. The technical extravaganza hosts a variety of technical and non technical programmes, inclusive of all ages and fields of work as it breaks the boundaries of tech with innovative ideas.
