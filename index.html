ABOUT TAKSHAK

Takshak, MACE’s annual tech fest, derives its name from the giant five-headed serpent king. And just like the naga proudly rears its head, Takshak holds the label of being one among the premier techie meets of South India. The technical extravaganza hosts a variety of technical and non technical programmes, inclusive of all ages and fields of work as it breaks the boundaries of tech with innovative ideas. 

ABOUT MACE

Somewhere between the highlands of Munnar and the glitz and glam of Kochi lies the small town of Kothamangalam, home to MACE. Established in 1961, Mar Athanasius College of Engineering is one of Kerala’s top colleges, kindling the spirits of young engineers for over 60 years. With 5 fully fledged departments and a myriad of skilled staff, MACE guides students to spread their wings professionally, all while offering them a whole lot of valuable life lessons.
